Retrieves default Predefined Content items from a specific account.

### Request

| Method|  URL|
 |:--------|  :-----|
 |GET|  https://[{domain}](/agent-domain-domain-api.html)/api/configuration/defaults/engagement-window/canned-responses/ |

### Response

**Response Codes**

 |Code  |Description |
 |:-----|  :--------------|
 |200  |OK |
 |404|  Data Not Found|
